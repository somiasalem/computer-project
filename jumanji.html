Jumanji

Story:
Welcome to the Jungle is based on a magical video game that pulls players into its world, forcing them to live as their in-game characters. A group of teenagers suddenly find themselves inside the game, each taking on a completely different personality and abilities than in real life. To escape, they must complete a dangerous adventure full of challenges, relying on teamwork and adapting to their new identities. The film explores themes like self-discovery, cooperation, and how people can grow when they step outside their comfort zones.

    Actors:
  • Dwayne Johnson (Dr. Smolder Bravestone)
  • Kevin Hart (Franklin “Mouse” Finbar)
  • Jack Black (Professor Sheldon)
  • Karen Gillan (Ruby Roundhouse)
